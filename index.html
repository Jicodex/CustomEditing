Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer an “unlimited video editing” package?

No — and intentionally. No one can truly deliver “unlimited” editing. That term is a marketing trick. Teams that advertise it still restrict you with:

  • One-at-a-time request queues
  • Slow or inconsistent turnarounds
  • Vague “fair use” caps
  • Editors overloaded with too many clients

You don’t get unlimited output — you get chaos and expectations no team can realistically meet. We take the opposite approach: clear limits, transparent output, and a workflow engineered for consistent quality every single month.

What do you mean by “Unlimited Revisions”?

Every video you submit gets revised until it’s right — no caps, no extra fees, no penalties. We perfect one active project at a time. Once that video is approved, we immediately begin the next.

What is Sequential Video Editing?

Submit multiple videos at once — we’ll handle them one at a time in sequence. You approve one → we start the next. This keeps quality sharp, communication clean, and turnaround predictable.

What is your turnaround time?

Typical turnaround is 2–3 business days once all assets are received. Operating hours: Monday–Friday, 10am–5pm MT Videos submitted after 10am MT, on weekends, or major U.S. holidays roll over to the next business day.

How do I provide feedback?

All communication happens inside your portal — clean, organized, and trackable.

Do you offer stock video or audio?

Yes. High-quality, royalty-free stock is included. Stock is capped at 20% of the final edit to keep your content authentic.

Do you offer lower thirds or transitions?

Absolutely. Modern, customizable lower thirds and transitions are available by request.

Do you offer animations?

Yes — custom animations are available on a per-project basis. Request a quote inside your portal.

Is there a long-term contract?

No contracts. Month-to-month — just like CustomThumbnails.com.

How do I communicate with my editor?

Directly inside your portal. One place. Zero chaos.

What are your requirements for raw footage?

Raw footage should be 30 minutes or less. If it’s longer, message us before submitting — we’ll help you plan the best workflow.

How long can my final video be?

Final edits can be up to 25 minutes. If you need longer, contact us beforehand so we can allocate the appropriate time and resources.

What types of videos do you edit?

We don’t edit:

  • Wedding videos
  • Explicit content

Beyond that, we’ve handled nearly every creator format.

Do you offer voiceovers?

You can provide your own VO, but we don’t currently offer voiceover services.

Do you create short-form clips?

Yes — available as an add-on. Every clip is handcrafted by a real editor (no AI auto-cutting), optimized for impact and retention.

Do you offer color correction?

Yes. Basic color correction and grading (exposure, white balance, tone) is included.

Do credits roll over?

Credits do not roll over. Example: If your plan includes 4 edits and you only use 3 before your billing date, the unused credit expires and your count resets. This keeps production predictable, ensures you always work with the same editor each month, and encourages consistent creation — which is exactly what helps channels gain momentum on YouTube.
